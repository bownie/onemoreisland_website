---

A contemporary novel about two brothers, a fractured family, and a man who discovers at fifty that it's still possible to begin again, One More Islands is set between the City of London and the Kent coast. It explores the stories families tell, the secrets they keep, and the difficult work of finding your way back to the people you've lost.

The events take place more than ten years before Human Software but in the same fictional port town of Sandport in South East Kent and also featuring end of the century London and Essex as its locations.

Richard W. Bown is a writer and software engineer who loves to explore life, work and the stories that every day brings. As a lifelong computer programmer, he’s worked in large companies throughout his thirty-year career, taking regular breaks to work on creative projects. One More Islands is his second novel.

Human Software his debut was published in 2025.
