One More Island is a contemporary novel about two brothers, a fractured family, and a man who discovers at fifty that it's still possible to begin again. Set between the City of London and the Kent coast, it explores the stories families tell, the secrets they keep, and the difficult work of finding your way back to the people you've lost.

One More Island is the second novel by Richard W. Bown. Human Software his debut novel was self published in 2025. 

One More Island is a prequel, set more than ten years before the first in the same fictional port town of Sandport in South East Kent. It also features end of the century London.

Richard W. Bown is a writer and software engineer who loves to explore life, work and the stories that every day brings. As a software engineer and manager, he’s worked in corporates throughout his thirty-year career, taking regular breaks to work on creative projects.
